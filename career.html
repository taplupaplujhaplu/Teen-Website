Career Preparation Guide for Teens

1. Self-Discovery

Discover what makes you unique! Try different hobbies, take personality tests, and reflect on your goals. What excites you the most?

Activity: Write down your top 3 interests and what you like about them.

2. Exploring Career Options

Explore career opportunities in tech, healthcare, or creative industries. Try virtual career fairs and internships!

Interactive: Use My Next Move for exploring careers.

3. Building Skills

Focus on soft skills like communication, leadership, and teamwork. Pick up hard skills like coding, writing, or designing.

Fun Fact: Learning new skills builds your brain power!

4. Education and Training

Education doesn’t mean just schools! Try online courses or vocational training too. Learn something new every day.

5. Career Planning Tools

Planning is the key! Build resumes, prepare portfolios, and do mock interviews to succeed in your dream job.

Quiz: What’s the most important section on a resume?

Correct! Experience is key.

6. Financial Literacy

Money management is super important. Learn how to save, budget, and invest your earnings wisely!

Saving ₹100/day can make you ₹36500 richer in a year!

7. Mental Health and Work-Life Balance

Good mental health is key to success. Try yoga, mindfulness, and take breaks to recharge your mind!

8. Networking and Mentorship

Meet new people, learn from mentors, and grow your circle of inspiration. Networking is like planting seeds of opportunity!

9. Entrepreneurship and Innovation

Turn your ideas into reality! Start small ventures or side projects to practice business skills early.

10. Digital Presence and Online Safety

Create a safe and professional online image. Avoid oversharing and keep your passwords secure!
